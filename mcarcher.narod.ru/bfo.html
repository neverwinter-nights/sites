Battle for Ovaron

"Battle for Ovaron" is a non-AI (PvP) multiplayer mission for Arma 2.
Gamestyle: PvP without respawn.
Playable slots: 64 NATO, 64 Russia and 14 UN units, 142 total.
More information in the documentation, which is included into archive.
